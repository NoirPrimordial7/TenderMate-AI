## Style guidance

- Hindi uses clear contemporary product language and avoids bureaucratic Sanskrit where a familiar term is clearer.
- Marathi uses natural action forms such as “करा” and “निवडा”; it does not copy Hindi sentence order.
- “Tender” may appear as “निविदा” in explanatory copy; source titles and official procurement quotations remain untouched.
- “Upload & open workspace” is translated as an upload action followed by opening the workspace, never as a promise that AI analysis has started.
